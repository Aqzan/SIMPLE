Sipadan Island

Kuala Koh Kelantan National Park, located in the Malaysian state of Kelantan, boasts pristine rainforests and diverse wildlife, offering visitors an immersive experience in nature conservation and ecotourism.

Location: Situated in the state of Kelantan, Malaysia, Kuala Koh Kelantan National Park boasts lush rainforests, pristine rivers, and diverse wildlife.

Attractions:

  • Guided jungle treks through dense rainforests.
  • Spectacular waterfalls, including Lata Rek.
  • Wildlife spotting, with opportunities to see Malayan tigers and Asian elephants.
  • Cultural experiences with indigenous Orang Asli communities, offering insights into their traditions and way of life.
